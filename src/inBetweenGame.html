In Between Game

In between is a betting game where 2 cards are shown and you're given a random card And you'll have to guess if your card is between the 2 cards that are shown. If your card is between the 2 cards. Then you'll win otherwise you lose. If the 2 cards are equal. You'll guess if your card is higher or lower. If you guess right you'll win. And if your card is the same as the 2 cards. You win the jackpot either you choose higher or lower
